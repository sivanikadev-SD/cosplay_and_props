Who We Are

Our Story

Born from a passion for anime and the art of making the fictional real.

Origin

Founded in
A Bedroom Workshop

Neon Prop Studio began in 2019 when founder Kenji Mori—frustrated with the lack of high-quality anime props on the market—3D-printed his first Gundam sword in a 10x10 bedroom studio. Word spread at Anime Expo 2020, and by 2022, the studio had a team of 12 dedicated smiths.

What started as a passion project became a global workshop shipping to 15+ countries, producing 500+ props, and building a community of 12,000+ cosplay enthusiasts who push the boundaries of what's possible.

Methodology

Three Forge Pillars

The core values that guide every single commission.

Innovation

We push the boundaries of what's buildable—integrating IoT, reactive LEDs, OLED panels, and articulated joints into props that feel like they crossed dimensions.

Precision

Every millimeter matters. We obsess over source material, using photogrammetry and 3D scanning to achieve 1:1 accuracy before a single gram of resin is printed.

Community

We're a community-first studio. From Discord design sessions to free blueprint drops, we believe the best props are made together, not in isolation.
