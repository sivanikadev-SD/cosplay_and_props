Workflow

The 6-Step Forge

Our battle-tested pipeline, from first sketch to your front door.

01
Blueprint

Full 2D projection before any work begins. We capture size, weight, and every key visual feature.

02
3D Modeling

ZBrush & Blender digital sculpting with integrated routing for internal electronics and structural supports.

03
Precision Print

8K SLA resin for ultra-fine details, industrial Hyper-PLA for core structural components.

04
Assembly

Carbon fiber & aluminum internal structures fused with industrial bonding agents for maximum durability.

05
Master Painting

Automotive-grade paints, multi-layer weathering, and UV-stable clear coat for a studio-quality finish.

06
Delivery

Stress-tested, photographed, packed in a padded custom case. Tracked & insured worldwide shipping.
